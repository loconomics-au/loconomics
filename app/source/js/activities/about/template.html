Loconomics empowers independent professionals with technology, shared ownership, and community. Whatever your specialty, we've created a networking and booking platform that puts you first.

We're not millionaires. We're no big tech company. We're a cooperative, meaning the better one of our owners does, the better all of us do. Rethink the way you're told to run your business: a cooperative model benefits you and your local community.

Loconomics is dedicated to our members, who provide helpful, creative, and meaningful services to the Bay Area and beyond.

Our Mission

Loconomics Australia's mission is to use technology, shared ownership, and community to strengthen local economies.

We provide tools, marketing, and community to our owners, empowering them to thrive in their work as local service professionals.

We provide their clients an app where they can discover and directly schedule local service professionals safely and easily, without middle-men.

Cooperative Model

A cooperative model means that every professional is an owner and has an equal vote in the election of the Loconomics board, consisting of fellow professionals, and in other major decisions. It also means that the profits generated by Loconomics will be returned to the owners, in proportion to their participation in generating profits.

Staff trusteeship

Tired of climbing a ladder to nowhere? Loconomics is taking a different approach to work. Staff trusteeship is a model of organizational governance that views all staff members of the cooperative as trustees of its mission. Staff trusteeship may emerge as a best practice in the governance of cooperatives with a large and geographically dispersed membership base. While the cooperative exists to benefit its members, it may be the full-time staff of the cooperative – not the Board of Directors – that is most capable and available to be directly accountable to the large membership base.

In comparison to staff of conventional businesses and organizations, all staff in a staff trusteeship cooperative have heightened responsibility, and, as such, heightened influence within the cooperative. In order to carry out their heightened responsibilities, each staff member will have relatively equal powers to that of other staff members. Each staff member will have opportunities to influence the activities of the cooperative and to influence their realm of work within the cooperative. The Staff Trustees shall use an internal governance/operational model, such as holacracy, that distributes power among them, ensuring a great deal of agency for each staff member.
