Set-up a Stripe account for payouts

Client payments will be disbursed directly to your existing Stripe account you setup here. If you do not have a Stripe account, a new one will be created linked to your email address you used when signing up for Loconomics. You will be responsible for transfering payments to your bank from Stripe.
On clicking the button below, you will be taken to the Stripe onboarding website to either link your existing Stripe account or create a new one.
